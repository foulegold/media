Logistics coordination involves offering planning, execution and monitoring of goods wending their way through both domestic and international supply chains. Coordination is needed between transportation, inventory management, communication with suppliers and customs compliance among others.

## **Documentation and Regulatory Compliance**

International trade is commitment-based and regulated by both customs and regulatory authorities. Mistakes or incomplete paperwork can lead to penalties or delays in shipments.

### **Essential Trade Documents**

- **Bill of Lading (BOL)**
- **Commercial Invoice**
- **Packing List**
- **Certificate of Origin**
- **Import/Export Licenses**
- **Insurance Certificates**

For correctness and timely clearance in customs, logistics providers handle the preparation, checking, and lodging of these documents.
