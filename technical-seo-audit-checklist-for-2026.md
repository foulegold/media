## **HTTPS and Security Standards**

Security, is still a trust and ranking factor.

Ensure a valid SSL certificate, no mixed content issues, and correct redirects from HTTP to HTTPS. As well as reviewing any potential vulnerabilities including malware and outdated plugins.

## **Duplicate Content and Canonicalization**

Search engines also get confused and ranking authority gets split in the case of duplicate content.

Common causes include HTTP vs HTTPS duplication, WWW vs non-WWW versions, and parameterized URLs. Consolidate signals using canonical tags and proper redirects.

## **Internal Linking Structure**

Internal linking helps to spread authority across page and makes navigation easier.

Best practices include interlinking to your content from authoritative pages, using descriptive anchor text, and avoiding excessive linking.

## **JavaScript SEO and Rendering**

JavaScript frameworks are ubiquitous in modern websites. But rendering it incorrectly can obstruct search engines.

Audit areas include ensuring server-side or dynamic rendering when appropriate, testing rendering with URL inspection tools, and avoiding hidden content issues.

## **Redirect Management**

Inconvenient redirection destroys user experience and SEO equity.

| Redirect Type | Purpose | SEO Impact |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 301 | Permanent redirect | Carries link equity |
| 302 | Found / Temporary redirect | Unable to completely pass authority |
| 307 | Temporary HTTP/1.1 redirect | Similar to 302 |

Ensure no redirect chains, no redirect loops, and that old URLs properly redirect to relevant pages.

## **404 Errors and Broken Links**

Dead links are bad for credibility and waste crawl budget.

Actions include identifying 404 errors regularly, redirecting high-value broken URLs, and removing internal broken links.

## **Hreflang Tags and International SEO**

For those who have a website that is targeting multiple regions or languages, implementing hreflang correctly will avoid duplicate content issues.

Ensure language and region codes are fixed, self-referencing hreflang tags are implemented, and consistency exists across language versions.

## **Log File Analysis**

Log file analysis gives you direct insight into how search engines crawl your site.

Review crawl frequency, crawl errors, and unnecessary bot activity. This sophisticated technique exposes technical pitfalls that conventional tools can overlook.

## **Crawl Budget Optimization**

Crawl efficiency is crucial to large websites.

Reduce crawl waste by eliminating thin pages, blocking low-value parameters, and consolidating duplicate content.
