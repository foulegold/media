## How the Bull Rush Slot Works

Bull Rush is a 5-reel video slot that typically features 50 paylines. It utilizes Konami’s signature **"Action Stacked Symbols"** mechanic. This means that before each spin, a random symbol is chosen to fill a stack of positions on the reels. This increases the chance of landing a full screen of matching symbols, which is where the game’s massive payout potential lies.

### Key Game Features:

- **The Bull (Wild):** The Bull symbol substitutes for all other symbols (except the Scatter). In the base game, it helps connect wins, but in the bonus round, it becomes a powerhouse.
- **The Scatter (Shrine/Gate):** Landing 3, 4, or 5 of these triggers the Free Games feature.
- **High Volatility:** This machine is not for the faint of heart. It is designed to have "dry spells" followed by huge spikes in winnings.
